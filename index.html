Design Thinking Outputs

Our Proposed Solutions

01
🗑️
Colour-Coded Segregation Bins
Place clearly labelled bins — green (recyclable), red (non-recyclable), brown (compostable) — at all key campus points including classrooms, hostels, and canteens.
02
📱
Digital Awareness Campaign
QR codes near bins, WhatsApp nudges, and weekly waste data dashboards to keep the campus community engaged and informed about their waste habits.
03
🌾
On-Campus Composting Unit
Convert canteen and hostel food waste (~200 kg/day estimated) into compost for campus gardens, reducing landfill load and transport cost significantly.
04
📊
Waste Monitoring System
Log daily waste by department, track trends over time, and give management real data (not just 2018 records) to optimise collection schedules and resources.
05
🏆
Zero Waste Club & Incentives
Form a student-run Zero Waste Club, run monthly competitions between departments, and reward the cleanest department — turning responsibility into pride.
06
📋
Policy Enforcement
Introduce a clear campus waste policy with regular audits, strict monitoring by maintenance staff, and visible accountability at all levels of management.
